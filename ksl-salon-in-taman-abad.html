Frequently Asked Questions About KSL Salon

What are your operating hours at KSL City Mall and do I need an appointment?
We're open daily from 10:30 AM to 9:30 PM at our KSL City Mall outlet (Level 3). While we accept walk-ins, we strongly recommend making an appointment to avoid waiting times, especially during weekends and evenings. You can book by calling us at +607-123-4567 or through our WhatsApp booking service. We require a 50% deposit for appointments during peak hours (Friday-Sunday).
How much does a basic hair cut and color service cost?
Our basic haircut starts from RM38 for ladies and RM28 for men. For hair coloring, prices start from RM188 depending on hair length and condition. This includes L'Oréal professional products and a basic wash. For shoulder-length hair with single color, you can expect to pay around RM188-238. Additional charges apply for hair longer than shoulder length (RM30-50 extra) or special techniques like balayage or highlights. We provide exact quotes during consultation.
How long does a full color treatment typically take at your salon?
A full color service usually takes 2-3 hours depending on your hair length, desired color, and current hair condition. If you're going from dark to light hair, it might take longer or require multiple sessions. We recommend allocating at least 3 hours for your first color appointment to ensure proper consultation and processing time. Touch-ups typically take 1.5-2 hours.
Do you offer any warranty or guarantee for your color services?
Yes, we provide a 7-day color guarantee. If you're unhappy with your color results, return within 7 days and we'll adjust it free of charge. This applies to technical issues only (like uneven color or unexpected results) and doesn't cover changes of mind about the chosen color. We also provide detailed aftercare instructions to maintain your color's vibrancy longer.
What hair treatment brands do you use and are your stylists certified?
We exclusively use professional brands including L'Oréal Professional, Wella, and Shiseido for our services. All our stylists are certified and regularly attend training sessions with these brands. Our senior stylists have minimum 5 years experience and are certified by the Malaysian Hairdressing Association. We also have specialists for specific services like Korean volume rebonding and Japanese straightening treatments.
